It centralizes storage and management of Wikipedias structured data and provides access to it in various open formats. It is powered by the Wikibase software, which is build on top of the [MediaWiki platform](https://www.mediawiki.org/). ### Current involvement I am working on the [Wikibase software](https://www.mediawiki.org/wiki/Wikibase) as developer for [Wikimedia Deutchland](https://www.wikimedia.de/).
